---

layout: recipe
title: Sausage Gravy


ingredients:
- 1/2 lb breakfast sausage
- 2 tbps & 2 tsp flour
- 1 1/2 to 2 cup milk

directions:
- Cook sausage in pan
- Add flour to sausage and grease and mix into a roux
- Incorpoate milk
- Salt and Pepper to taste

---
